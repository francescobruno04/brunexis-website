About Brunexis

Built so no customer ever waits on hold.

Brunexis is an AI voice team for home service businesses. We help roofers, HVAC techs, plumbers, and restoration crews turn every ringing phone into a booked job — without hiring another receptionist.

Who we are

Brunexis is a small team of operators and engineers building practical AI for real-world businesses. We're not chasing hype — we're focused on making one thing work extremely well: a voice agent that answers your phone the way a great front-desk employee would.

Our mission

Every missed call is a customer who chose someone else. Our mission is simple: make sure that never happens to the businesses we serve. We build voice agents that are always on, always polite, always ready to qualify and book — so owners can focus on running crews, not the phone.

Why AI voice agents

Home service is a phone business. Leads call, not click. But most shops run into the same math: technicians can't answer while they're on a roof, office staff are booked with paperwork, and after-hours calls go straight to voicemail. Traditional call centers are expensive and inconsistent.

Modern voice AI changes that. A well-trained agent can answer instantly in a natural voice, ask the right qualifying questions, check your calendar, book the appointment, and hand off urgent issues to a human when needed. It works at 9 a.m. and at 2 a.m. It doesn't have bad days. And it costs a fraction of a full reception team.

Who we help

  • Roofing contractors juggling storm-season call spikes
  • HVAC companies handling emergency heat and cooling calls
  • Plumbing shops that can't afford to lose a burst-pipe lead
  • Restoration teams responding to water, fire, and mold damage 24/7
  • Growing home service brands scaling into new markets

If your business lives or dies by the phone, we built Brunexis for you.

How we work

We start by learning your business — services, service area, hours, pricing rules, common questions, and how you like your team to talk to customers. Then we train and tune a voice agent that sounds like your shop, not a generic bot. You keep control: what it books, how it qualifies, when it hands off to a human.
